## Geometry Goal

Make Darkness a post-VOID anti-floor where light is route structure: lit islands, shadow corridors, generator rooms, toll gates, occlusion walls and choices between spending light or taking dangerous dark shortcuts.

## Tasks

1. Generate a macro layout of safe light islands connected by dark corridors and optional generator/control rooms.
2. Make light scarcity spatial: visible safe zones, risky shortcuts, toll chokepoints and fallback pockets.
3. Use darkness to guide and threaten, not to make the player blind without choices.
4. Add motifs: light island, dead lamp row, generator room, shadow toll gate, black corridor, emergency stash.
5. Keep exits reachable without one mandatory rare item; provide at least one dangerous fallback route.
6. Run `npm run check` and visually check darkness readability.

## Done Means

The floor is scary because route planning spends light, not because the screen is simply too dark to play.
