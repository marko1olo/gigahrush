## Implementation

- Existing zhelemish ids were present: `zhelemish_raw`, `zhelemish_dried`, `zhelemish_boiled`.
- Added `zhelemish_skin` as a bounded timed `Entity.statuses` condition, sharing the existing status field used by govnyak conditions.
- Raw zhelemish applies a 180s skin and can trigger a bad reaction. Dried/boiled zhelemish apply a treated 150s skin.
- Benefits/costs:
  - incoming melee damage `x0.7`;
  - movement `x0.82`;
  - healing from normal usable items `x0.55`;
  - active water drain, higher during bad reaction;
  - raw bad reaction immediately costs water and PSI and publishes a witnessed event.
- Cure path: `antifungal_ointment` or `antibiotic` consumes normally and removes the skin without healing friction.
- Expiry is automatic and sleep/rest advances it because it uses simulated game time.
- HUD shows a compact active zhelemish line above the bottom bar.
- Inventory/stats panel shows duration, melee benefit, movement cost, healing cost and water drain.
- Save/load and floor transitions preserve active timed statuses.
- Events published: `player_status_applied`, `player_status_expired`, `player_status_cured`, `player_status_bad_reaction`.
