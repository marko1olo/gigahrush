## Core Metadata

Title:

`GIGAH|RUSH`

Short description:

`Browser survival horror / ARPG shooter about expeditions, factions, monsters and Samosbor inside an endless concrete apartment block.`

One-line hook:

`Pack food, water, ammo and documents, then survive a living concrete megastructure before Samosbor seals the floor.`

Developer blurb:

`GIGAH|RUSH is a zero-runtime-dependency TypeScript/Vite browser game built with WebGL, canvas HUD rendering, procedural textures, procedural sprites and procedural sound. It is playable directly in the browser as a single HTML5 build.`

Content warning:

`Contains survival horror themes, oppressive atmosphere, monsters, combat, death, corpses, blood, weapon use, sirens, confinement, body-horror-adjacent imagery and disturbing procedural events.`

Age / rating notes:

`Suggested portal rating: Teen / 12+ where self-rating is required. Not suitable for young children. Final rating should follow each portal's questionnaire and moderation rules.`

Controls:

- `WASD` - move.
- `Mouse` - look / aim.
- `E` - interact.
- `Space` or `Left Mouse Button` - attack.

Feature bullets:

- Browser survival horror / ARPG shooter with no install required.
- An unbounded concrete megastructure with rooms, corridors, lifts, factions, NPCs, containers, quests, production, rumors and Samosbor events in one persistent loop.
- Expedition structure: prepare food, water, ammo, medicine, documents, weapons or PSI tools before leaving the safer living area.
- Procedural and hand-crafted floors with faction territory, hostile routes, traders, monsters, anomalies and consequences.
- A-Life NPCs with identities, faction relationships, routines, inventory, death state and persistent effects between visits.
- Faction reputation, zone control, trading, stealing, quests, contracts, rumors, caravans, production and market pressure.
- Samosbor events can seal doors, push fog through cracks, wake monsters and alter the floor after the alarm.
- Browser saves, journal, map, faction screen and optional in-game Net Sphere terminal in supported builds.
- Procedural textures, sprites and sound; WebGL raycasting with canvas HUD.

Long description:

`GIGAH|RUSH is a browser survival horror / ARPG shooter about expeditions inside an endless Soviet-style concrete apartment block. The world is not a set of separate stages: flats, communal rooms, corridors, lifts, utility tunnels, markets, laboratories, ministry floors and void zones are tied into one dangerous route system.`

`You start in a safer living area, prepare food, water, ammo, medicine, documents, weapons or PSI tools, then follow a quest, rumor, contract or faction lead into hostile territory. Every trip asks the same practical question: what can you carry in, what can you bring back, and what happens if the floor changes before you return?`

`The building keeps moving without you. NPCs trade, sleep, fight, hide from Samosbor events and can die permanently. Factions react to what you do, zones can change control, prices can shift, caravans move resources, and consequences remain as dead bodies, empty containers, marks, rumors and reputation changes.`

`The central disaster is Samosbor. It can arrive with or without warning: doors seal, fog leaks through cracks, monsters become active, and parts of the floor can change after the alarm ends. The current browser build already includes preparation, expeditions, combat, trading, inventory, quests, factions, procedural floors, A-Life NPCs, browser saves and persistent consequences.`
